# Privacy Policy for Kaliki Browser

**Last Updated:** May 28, 2026

## Overview
Kaliki Browser ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile web browser for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect any personal data.** All browsing data (history, bookmarks, passwords) is stored locally on your device only.

### What stays on your device:
- Browsing history
- Bookmarks
- Saved passwords
- Downloaded files
- Cookie data
- Cache

### What we do NOT do:
- We do NOT send your browsing data to any server
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT sell your data
- We do NOT create user profiles
- We do NOT collect analytics about your browsing habits

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
We display ads via Google AdMob to support free access to the app. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising ID
- IP address (for ad targeting)
- General location (country/city level, from IP)

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### WebView (System Component)
The app uses Android System WebView for rendering web pages. Websites you visit may collect their own data according to their own privacy policies.

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Internet | Required to load web pages |
| Network State | Check if device is online |
| Storage (Android 9-) | Save downloaded files |
| Camera | Only when a website requests it (e.g., QR scan) |
| Microphone | Only when a website requests it (e.g., voice search) |
| Location | Only when a website requests it (e.g., maps) |
| Notifications | Download completion alerts |

All permissions that access camera, mic, or location are **requested at runtime** — you can deny them.

## Incognito Mode
When Incognito mode is enabled:
- No history is saved
- Third-party cookies are blocked
- Browsing data is cleared on exit

## Ad Blocker
Our built-in ad blocker works locally on your device. It does not send any data externally. Blocked ad/tracker counts are stored locally.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
